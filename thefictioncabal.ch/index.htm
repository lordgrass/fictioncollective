Introduction

The Fiction Cabal is a community of people interested in fictional universes, no matter the franchise. We, as a community started out on discord as a Star Wars themed discord server and now we do much, much more!

Beginnings

The conception of our community began with the creation of WhatIfAdrians YouTube Channel and the subsequent creation of his Discord server which would eventually be renamed to "The Fiction Collective" and later "The Fiction Cabal" by Enadasa. Early on, circa 2020, Enadasa then known as Grass would become the lead administrator.
